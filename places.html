Venice

A fabulous city. A city in the north-east of Italy. The administrative center of the Venice region and the territorial unit of the same name, equated to the province. Forms a commune divided into 6 self-governing districts. The patron saint of the city is the Apostle Mark, the evangelist, whose relics are kept here in St. Mark's Cathedral.
